🔑 Warden (Super Admin)

Full unrestricted access to inmate profiles, medical records, and audit logs. Can create, read, update, and delete any record.

🩺 Medical Staff

Can view and update medical records (diagnosis & medications). Can view inmate profiles but cannot modify them.

🛡️ Security Staff

Can view inmate profiles and perform cell block transfers. Cannot access medical records or the audit log directly.

👤 Public Visitor

Read-only access to non-sensitive inmate information (name, alias, cell block). No access to medical or audit data.

⚡ Auto-Record Engine

Every create, update, delete, and read action on inmate or medical data is automatically captured as an immutable security log entry.

🔒 Security Gateway

Three layers of protection: JWT identity verification, role-based access control, and rate limiting to prevent abuse or brute-force attempts.

🛡️ Security & Access Control

The Arkham Registry implements a Zero Trust adjacent philosophy. No request enters the core system without passing through the Security Gateway. JWT (JSON Web Tokens) ensure that every actor is who they claim to be, while Group-Based Permissions ensure they can only perform actions authorized for their specific role (e.g., Medical Staff cannot initiate inmate transfers).

🚨 The Admission Engine

Our robust new intake guardrails. Before any new inmate is registered, the Admission Engine enforces strict facility rules: it verifies the target Cell Block Capacity to prevent overcrowding, and enforces Initial Diagnosis Presence to ensure immediate psychological evaluation, generating both physical placement and medical files simultaneously.

📋 Core Modules & Business Logic

The system is divided into functional domains. Inmate Management handles the lifecycle of prisoners and their physical location within the Asylum. Medical Records handles sensitive health data, isolated under strict privacy controls. User Group Management handles role-based clearances for all staff members. Finally, our Audit & Watchtower module serves as the eyes of the administration, providing full transparency into system usage.

📜 The Immutable Audit Trail

Security is enforced not just at the gate, but through constant monitoring. Every significant event—whether it's Dr. Leland updating a diagnosis or Guard Boles moving an inmate—is captured by the Auto-Record Engine. Furthermore, our Transfer Safety Engine reads these logs to prevent guards from moving inmates if they haven't been recently cleared by a doctor.
